What is Salesforce Marketing Cloud?

Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) is an all-in-one digital marketing platform that helps you manage, automate, and personalize customer interactions across email, SMS, social media, apps, and the web. It features a modular architecture built around:

Studios

Tools like Email Studio and Mobile Studio for managing messaging across channels.

Builders

Tools like Journey Builder to automate multi-touch customer journeys and Audience Builder for segmentation.

AI-powered personalization

Predict behavior and optimize performance with Salesforce Einstein.

Data & Analytics

Get real-time insights and measure marketing effectiveness.
