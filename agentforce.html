What is Agentforce?

Agentforce is a modern customer support platform that unifies automation, AI, and human interaction to deliver seamless service experiences across chat, email, phone, and more. It is purpose-built to reduce resolution time, boost agent efficiency, and enhance customer satisfaction through:

AI-powered Virtual Agents

Automate first-level support to handle routine inquiries at scale.

Unified Agent Console

Equip agents with real-time insights, customer context, and tools in one streamlined interface.

Smart Routing & Escalation

Ensure each case reaches the right agent based on skill, availability, and urgency.

Analytics & Feedback

Monitor service quality, response trends, and agent performance with built-in dashboards.
